Rebecca Yarros

It’s a winner. The reviews here are fortunately all true. Dynamite… New-Adult/College-life has never been this darn exciting. From the first chapter you are transported into utter chaos, mayhem and madness— with danger at every corner. A instant “hate” relationship between the two love/leads characters, with a host of amazingly secondary characters, that make up this phenomenal saga.
The first half of this book is unbearably slow and has its protagonist change from an intelligent, somewhat sassy warrior to an insufferable and insecure hypocrite. Every other chapter has Xaden and Violet fighting over some sort of misunderstanding or secret, followed by an extremely graphic make out session.
The excitement, the magic, the battles and love story all come together to create such a scene in your head that it’s like a movie unfolding as you read. Her characters are incredibly personable and realistic, with diseases and handicaps and different lifestyles all being accepted and normal.
